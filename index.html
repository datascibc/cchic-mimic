CC-HIC Database

Critical Care - Health Informatics Collaborative (CC-HIC) is an NIHR-supported UK collaboration between the five Biomedical Research Centres based at Cambridge, Guy's & St Thomas', Imperial, Oxford and UCL. Ten hospitals with fifteen intensive care units have created a repository containing data from 15,000 admissions with more than 120 million observations
