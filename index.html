Datathon 2016, 3-4 December 2016

This is the 4th annual Critical Care data weekend, bringing together teams of clinicians and data scientists from London and Boston, USA. Critical Care Data London invites clinicians and data scientists, experts and novices to come and explore two iconic clinical data repositories with talks from experts in electronic health records, 'big data' and database design.
